payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via Visa, MasterCard, American Express Credit and Debit Cards,Netbanking, EMI and also ITZ Cash Cards. We also accept payment via "Cash on Delivery" at the time of the delivery of the product.

Is it safe to shop online using my Debit card or Credit Card?

All Credit Card, Debit Card, ITZ Cash Card and net banking transactions are processed over a secure encrypted connection. Also, we are verified by Norton as a Safe Site. So no worries here!

Is there a purchase limit for a Cash On Delivery purchase?

Yes, Cash On Delivery option is available on purchases up to a maximum limit of Rs. 5000, beyond which purchases can only be made using Netbanking, ITZ Cash Card, EMI, Debit Card or Credit Card.

What should I do if my payment fails?

In case of a payment failure, please retry ensuring: Information passed on to payment gateway is accurate i.e. account details, billing address, password (for net banking) Your Internet connection is not disrupted in the process. If your account has been debited after a payment failure, it is normally rolled back to your bank account within 7 business days. You can add your complaint by filling the below form or please give us a call with your order number for any clarification.
