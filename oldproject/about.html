gallery

Design Bombs is a gallery of the best web designs online - a CSS gallery, a site for inspiration - and generally strives to be a resource for designers who are looking to sharpen their craft.

Design Bombs was originally created by Soh Tanaka, an LA-based web designer. A few years later Max Spiker took over the site and kept it running with the help of Chad Mueller.

 In November 2013 the site was redesigned and updated with more of a focus on web design articles and more in-depth resources.
